Pizzaghetti

Description

Pizza-ghetti is a combination of two foods, Pizza and Spaghetti (or Pasta). This idea originated in Quebec Canada. The original Pizza-ghetti consists of a slice of pizza and a serving of pasta served on the same dish side by side. This dish spread rapidly to the US and became as one.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package spaghetti
  • 1 (28 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) package sliced pepperoni
  • 1 (6 ounce) can sliced black olives
  • 1 (8 ounce) package sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 8 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
